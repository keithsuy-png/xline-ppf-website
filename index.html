Got Questions?

Paint Protection Film (PPF) FAQs

+ What is PPF?

Paint Protection Film is a virtually invisible polyurethane film applied to your vehicle's exterior. It acts as a physical barrier against stone chips, scratches, bug splatter, and road debris.

+ Does PPF affect the appearance of my vehicle?

No. High-quality PPF is optically clear and designed to maintain your vehicle's original colour and finish. Gloss films enhance shine, while satin films create a smooth matte appearance.

+ Should I get ceramic coating or PPF?

Both offer unique benefits. PPF provides the strongest protection against physical damage, while ceramic coating enhances gloss, hydrophobicity, and ease of cleaning. Many customers choose to combine both for maximum protection and finish.

+ How long does PPF last?

With proper care, premium PPF can last for many years. It is engineered for long-term durability and resistance to environmental wear.

+ Can PPF be removed?

PPF can be applied to most painted surfaces on any vehicle, including cars, motorcycles, and performance vehicles. It can also be safely removed by a professional without damaging the underlying paint.

+ Will PPF turn yellow over time?

High-quality modern films are designed to resist yellowing and maintain clarity over time when properly maintained.
